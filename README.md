# 📜 Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) Development

## 📌 Project Overview
Developed a comprehensive Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) for a mock technology organization to ensure the secure and responsible use of IT resources.

The policy aligns with industry standards including NIST SP 800-53 and ISO/IEC 27001:2022 to support regulatory compliance and risk mitigation.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Define acceptable and unacceptable use of company IT assets  
- Reduce security risks caused by user behavior  
- Establish clear guidelines for employees  
- Support incident reporting and accountability  

---

## 🛠️ Frameworks & Standards
- NIST SP 800-53  
- ISO/IEC 27001:2022  

---

## 📂 Key Policy Areas
- Acceptable Use of Systems  
- Prohibited Activities  
- Internet & Email Usage  
- Password & Authentication Requirements  
- Data Protection & Confidentiality  
- Incident Reporting Procedures  

---

## 🧠 Implementation Approach
- Identified common organizational security risks  
- Mapped policy controls to industry standards  
- Structured policy for clarity and usability  
- Focused on real-world enforceability  

---

## 📊 Outcome
The policy provides a structured framework for governing user behavior, reducing insider threats, and strengthening overall organizational security posture.
